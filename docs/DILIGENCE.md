# Diligence: ABLTV Technical Moat

## Active Boundary Layer Thrust Vectoring (ABLTV)

The core technical advantage of Fluidity Marine is the elimination of mechanical rudders in favor of digital hydrodynamic modulation.

### Competitive Advantages:
1. **Parasitic Drag Elimination:** Standard outboards lose up to 12% of thrust to rudder surface friction. We reclaim this energy.
2. **Zero Moving Parts (External):** By using a transom jet array, we remove the #1 failure point in commercial and recreational boating.
3. **Electronic Precision:** Turning response is modulated by software at the millisecond level, allowing for "Dynamic Positioning" without complex thrusters.

### The Transom Array:
Our system utilizes a synchronized array of 7 thrust nodes along the transom line. Steering is achieved through differential thrust/drag modulation, effectively "warping" the boundary layer of the hull's interface with the water.
